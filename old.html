About me

I am a passionate Software Engineer for discovering solutions that help people to get better lives. I have been working with technology since 2008 as a Software Developer and Architect. My focus is on mainly Java web applications, web development, and enterprise solutions.

Currently, I work as a Consulting Software Engineer at Phoebus Tecnology and Tech Leader at the State Court of Paraiba. In 2019, as Tech Leader, I have taught the Agile Principles to the team, and we have achieved an increase of 300% in the development team productivity. Thus, we have faster development and better QA approval. Moreover, we have gotten a 2-years app project off the drawer for both Android/iOS systems. These apps are for procedural consultation, including document viewing, and they are having a daily average of 200 access/hour, which means 20 times the in-loco service capacity.

In my academic background, I graduated in Software Development* for Federal Institute of Education, Science and Technology – IFRN/Brazil (*summa cum laude). I have earned my Master’s Degree in Computer Engineering for Federal University of Rio Grande do Norte – UFRN/Brazil.
